Welcome to a demonstration of the Monte Carlo Algorithm!

Here you can compete head-to-head against an AI trained to use the Monte Carlo Algorithm to play the board game Battleship. This algorithm takes a straightforward approach - it looks at a large sample of randomly generated possible game states and selects the move where a ship is most likely to be.

You'll first play the game yourself, and then have the option to watch the AI play the same board configuration. An extra game board will appear giving you a glance into what the AI sees as it's playing. Before each move, the AI generates a heat map of how likely a ship is on each square by aggregating data from hundreds of thousands of possible ship configurations given the current game state (ie. opponent's ships remaining, previous hits and misses) and selects the square where a ship was present in the most configurations.

See if you can finish in less turns than the AI!
